Mauricio Daros Andrade

 

Postdoctoral Researcher
School of Applied Mathematics, Fundação Getulio Vargas
Email: mauricio.andrade@fgv.br

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the School of Applied Mathematics (EMAp) at Fundação Getulio Vargas (FGV). I completed my PhD in Statistics in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the Wharton School, where I worked under the supervision of Bhaswar B. Bhattacharya. Before that, I obtained my Master's in Mathematical Economics at the National Institute for Pure and Applied Mathematics (IMPA) and my Bachelors in Mathematics at the Universidade Federal de Minas Gerais (UFMG).

Research Interests

  • Combinatorial Probability.

  • Nonparametric Statistics.

  • Keywords: graph limit theory, higher-order U-statistics, chaos expansions, phylogenetic trees.
